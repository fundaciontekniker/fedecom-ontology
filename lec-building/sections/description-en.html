LEC-Building: Energy Communities and Buildings Characterization module for the FEDECOM Ontology: Description back to ToC

The LEC‑Building module of the FEDECOM ontology offers a unified model for characterising Local Energy Communities through their sites and the buildings included in them. It represents core building features—such as area, typology, structure, location, circuits, rooms, and associated energy assets, enabling a consistent community‑level modelling.
